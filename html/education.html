Education

2010 - 2017

Taraz "Bilim-Innovtaion" Lyceum

2017 - 2022

I studied at Taraz BIL (Bilim-Innovtaion Lyceum) from 2017 to 2022, and it was a crucial period in my life. This school provided me with not only excellent academic knowledge but also valuable lessons in brotherhood and behavior. Living in the dormitory was one of the most interesting and memorable experiences, where I built strong friendships and learned independence. The sense of community, discipline, and the deep bonds I formed during my time at KTL have left a lasting impact on my character and future aspirations.

SDU

2022 - current

I am currently studying at Suleyman Demirel University (SDU), and it has been a rewarding experience so far. Decode

2023 - 2024

In 2023, I joined Decode, an online programming school focused on web development. It was a transformative experience that equipped me with practical skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and backend development. Decode’s structured curriculum and hands-on projects allowed me to deepen my understanding of web technologies and build real-world applications. This program was crucial in advancing my coding abilities and gave me the confidence to pursue my goal of becoming a backend developer.
